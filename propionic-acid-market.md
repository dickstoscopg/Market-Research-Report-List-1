<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Propionic Acid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oxo Process</li><li>Reppe Process</li><li>By-product Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The propionic acid market is primarily categorized into three processes: the Oxo process, Reppe process, and By-product process. </p><p>The Oxo process involves the hydroformylation of ethylene, producing propionaldehyde, which is subsequently oxidized to yield propionic acid. The Reppe process synthesizes propionic acid from acetylene and carbon monoxide, offering a more direct route. Conversely, the By-product process involves generating propionic acid as a secondary product during the manufacturing of other chemicals, leveraging existing production facilities and reducing costs.</p></p>
