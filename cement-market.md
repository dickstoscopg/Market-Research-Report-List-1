<p><p>The cement market encompasses various types, each serving distinct purposes. Portland cement, the most common, is essential for general construction due to its strength and durability. White cement offers aesthetic appeal for architectural projects, distinguished by its color and finish. Hydraulic cement is recognized for its ability to set and harden in water, making it suitable for underwater construction. Alumina cement, known for its quick-setting properties and high heat resistance, is utilized in specialized applications, particularly in refractory materials.</p></p>
