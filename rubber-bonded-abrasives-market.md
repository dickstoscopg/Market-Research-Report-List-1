<p><p>The rubber bonded abrasives market is divided into two main types: natural rubber bonded abrasives and synthetic rubber bonded abrasives. Natural rubber bonded abrasives are derived from latex and offer flexibility and resilience, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, synthetic rubber bonded abrasives are engineered from polymers, providing enhanced durability, heat resistance, and consistent performance over time. Both types are utilized in industries like metalworking, woodworking, and automotive for grinding, cutting, and polishing tasks.</p></p>
