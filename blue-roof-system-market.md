<p><ul><li>Active Blue Roof System</li><li>Passive Blue Roof System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Blue Roof System market is categorized into two main types: Active and Passive Blue Roof Systems. Active Blue Roof Systems involve mechanisms to control water retention and drainage actively, utilizing sensors and pumps to manage stormwater dynamically. In contrast, Passive Blue Roof Systems rely on gravity and natural drainage processes, absorbing and releasing rainwater without mechanical intervention. Both types aim to mitigate urban flooding, enhance water management, and promote sustainable practices in urban architecture and design.</p></p>
