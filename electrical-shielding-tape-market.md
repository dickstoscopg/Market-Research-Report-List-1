<p><p>The Electrical Shielding Tape market comprises two primary types: Adhesive Shielding Tapes and Non-Adhesive Shielding Tapes. Adhesive Shielding Tapes feature a sticky backing, allowing for easy application and effective bonding to surfaces, making them ideal for tight spaces and irregular shapes. Non-Adhesive Shielding Tapes, on the other hand, rely on mechanical fastening or other methods for installation, which can be essential in applications requiring reusability or where adhesive residue is a concern. Both types serve to protect electronic components from electromagnetic interference.</p></p>
