<p><strong>The Chemical Zirconium Oxide Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydrothermal Method</li><li>Precipitation Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemical zirconium oxide market includes production methods such as the hydrothermal method and the precipitation method. The hydrothermal method involves synthesizing zirconium oxide under high-pressure and high-temperature conditions, resulting in high purity and crystalline structure. In contrast, the precipitation method involves the chemical reaction of zirconium salts to form zirconium hydroxide, which is then calcined to produce zirconium oxide. Both methods are crucial for producing various grades of zirconium oxide used in ceramics, electronics, and other industrial applications.</p></p>
