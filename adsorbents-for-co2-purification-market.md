<p><p>The CO2 purification market features three primary types of adsorbents: carbon-based adsorbents, zeolites, and metal oxide sorbents. Zeolites are crystalline aluminosilicates with a porous structure, providing selective adsorption capabilities. Each type serves unique applications in carbon capture and storage technologies.</p></p>
