<p><p>Concentrating Solar Power (CSP) systems harness sunlight to generate electricity through various technologies. Parabolic troughs use curved mirrors to focus sunlight onto fluid-filled tubes, while solar towers utilize a central receiver to capture sunlight from mirrors arranged around it. Linear Fresnel systems employ flat mirrors to concentrate sunlight onto pipes, whereas dish/engine systems feature parabolic dishes that concentrate sunlight to drive an engine. Each type has distinct operational characteristics, efficiencies, and applications, catering to diverse energy needs.</p></p>
