<p><p>The ornamental fish feed market is primarily divided into two types: live food and processed food. Live food includes organisms like brine shrimp, daphnia, and other small aquatic creatures, providing essential nutrients and stimulating natural feeding behaviors in fish. On the other hand, processed food consists of pellets, flakes, and granules, which are formulated to meet the nutritional needs of various fish species. Both types play crucial roles in promoting fish health, growth, and overall vitality in aquariums.</p></p>
