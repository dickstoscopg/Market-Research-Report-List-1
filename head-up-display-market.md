<p><p>The Head-Up Display (HUD) market is primarily divided into two types: Windshield Projected HUD and Combiner Projected HUD. Windshield Projected HUD integrates visuals directly onto the vehicle's windshield, offering a seamless and immersive driving experience with essential information displayed without obstructing the driver's view. In contrast, Combiner Projected HUD utilizes a smaller transparent screen that can be placed in the driver's line of sight, projecting vital data for easier accessibility while maintaining focus on the road. Both types enhance safety and convenience.</p></p>
