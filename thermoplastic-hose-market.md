<p><p>The thermoplastic hose market features various types, including twin hoses and multiple hoses. Twin hoses consist of two parallel tubes, typically used for applications requiring simultaneous fluid transfer, such as welding. Multiple hoses combine several tubes into a single assembly, allowing for the transport of diverse fluids or gases in one streamlined unit. Both configurations offer flexibility, lightweight design, and resistance to chemicals and abrasion, catering to various industries like automotive, construction, and manufacturing.</p></p>
