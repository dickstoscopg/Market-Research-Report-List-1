<p><p>The towel dryers market is categorized into two main types: electric and hydronic. Electric towel dryers utilize electric heating elements to warm towels, offering convenience and faster drying times. They are ideal for homes and smaller spaces. In contrast, hydronic towel dryers function by circulating heated water through pipes, providing a consistent heat source. This type is often integrated into home heating systems, making it energy-efficient for larger spaces. Both types enhance bathroom comfort and promote hygiene.</p></p>
