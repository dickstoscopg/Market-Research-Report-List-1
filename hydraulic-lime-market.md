<p><p>The hydraulic lime market is categorized into three main types based on the percentage of calcium oxide and hydraulic properties: NHL2, NHL3.5, and NHL5. NHL2, with lower strength, is suitable for delicate restoration work. NHL3.5, offering moderate strength, is commonly used for masonry and general construction. NHL5, the strongest variant, is ideal for demanding applications such as heavy-duty construction and structural repairs. Each type serves distinct functions, catering to varying project requirements and performance standards.</p></p>
