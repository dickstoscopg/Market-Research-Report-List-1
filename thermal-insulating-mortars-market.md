<p><p>The thermal insulating mortars market is divided into two main types: inorganic insulating mortars and organic insulating mortars. Inorganic insulating mortars are typically made from materials like cement and silica, offering high durability, fire resistance, and thermal stability, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. On the other hand, organic insulating mortars are primarily based on polymeric compounds, providing flexibility, ease of application, and better adhesion, ideal for controlled environments and energy-efficient constructions. Both types cater to specific insulation needs in various construction projects.</p></p>
