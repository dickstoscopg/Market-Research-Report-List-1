<p><p>The non-adhesive foam dressing market is categorized into two main types: without adhesive border and with adhesive border. Non-adhesive border dressings are designed to be used with additional fixation methods, allowing flexibility in application. They provide cushioning and moisture control but require external support to stay in place. In contrast, foam dressings with adhesive borders feature a built-in adhesive perimeter that secures the dressing directly to the skin, offering convenience while maintaining a barrier against contaminants. Both types are essential for wound care strategies.</p></p>
