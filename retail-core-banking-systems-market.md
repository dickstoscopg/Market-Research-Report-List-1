<p><p>Retail core banking systems can be categorized into three types: On-Premises, Managed, and Hybrid. On-Premises solutions are installed and maintained locally within a bank's infrastructure, offering full control but requiring significant IT resources. Managed services involve outsourcing the hosting and maintenance to a third-party provider, reducing the internal IT burden while ensuring expertise. Hybrid solutions combine elements of both, with core banking functions hosted on-premises and supplementary services or applications managed externally, enhancing flexibility and scalability.</p></p>
