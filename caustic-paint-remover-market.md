<p><p>The caustic paint remover market can be categorized into two main types: oily and paste formulations. Oily paint removers typically contain solvents and are effective for penetrating and dissolving tough paint layers, often offering a faster application. In contrast, paste-type removers have a thicker consistency, allowing them to cling to vertical surfaces and work on stubborn coatings for extended periods. Both types are used widely in residential and industrial settings for efficient paint removal across various surfaces.</p></p>
