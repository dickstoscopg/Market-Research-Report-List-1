<p><strong>The Inkjet Card Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Foaming</li><li>Micro Piezo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inkjet card printer market features two main types: Thermal Foaming and Micro Piezo. Thermal Foaming printers use heat to create bubbles in ink, propelling droplets onto the card surface; this method is often cost-effective and suitable for various printing tasks. In contrast, Micro Piezo technology utilizes mechanical pressure to eject ink from cartridges, allowing for precise droplet control and high-quality prints. Both types cater to diverse printing needs, from identification cards to personalized gifts, emphasizing quality and efficiency.</p></p>
