<p><ul><li>Vertical Type</li><li>Horizontal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Profile extrusion lines are categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on their orientation and manufacturing processes. Vertical type lines involve a vertical alignment in which materials are fed from above, allowing for efficient gravity-assisted flow. This type is ideal for producing long, complex profiles. In contrast, horizontal type lines have a lateral orientation, facilitating easier access and maintenance. Such setups are generally suited for producing a wide range of profiles, offering versatility in production for varied applications.</p></p>
