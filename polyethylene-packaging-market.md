<p><strong>The Polyethylene Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Density Polystyrene (HDPE)</li><li>Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE)</li><li>Locked Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyethylene packaging market comprises several types based on density and structure. High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) is known for its strength and resistance to moisture, making it suitable for containers, bottles, and industrial packaging. Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) is flexible and easily moldable, commonly used for bags and wraps. Locked Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) offers enhanced tensile strength and puncture resistance, making it ideal for stretch films and flexible packaging. Each type serves distinct applications, balancing durability, flexibility, and protection.</p></p>
