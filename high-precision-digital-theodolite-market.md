<p><p>The high-precision digital theodolite market is primarily divided into two types: optical and electronic theodolites. In contrast, electronic theodolites incorporate advanced digital technology, enabling faster computations, data storage, and communication with computer systems. This allows for enhanced precision, ease of use, and integration with GPS and other modern surveying tools, catering to a wider range of applications in construction and engineering.</p></p>
