<p><p>The Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease (GERD) therapeutics market encompasses various treatment types including antacids, pro-kinetic agents, H2 receptor blockers, and proton pump inhibitors (PPIs). Antacids neutralize stomach acid for quick relief. Pro-kinetic agents enhance gut motility to prevent reflux. H2 receptor blockers reduce gastric acid production, providing symptomatic relief. PPIs, a mainstay in GERD treatment, inhibit acid secretion for prolonged relief and healing. The market is driven by the increasing prevalence of GERD and evolving therapeutic options.</p></p>
