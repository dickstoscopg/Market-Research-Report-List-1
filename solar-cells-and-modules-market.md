<p><p>The solar cells and modules market features various technologies catering to different energy needs. Mono-Si (monocrystalline silicon) modules are known for their high efficiency and space efficiency. Multi-Si (polycrystalline silicon) modules are cost-effective but slightly less efficient. CdTe (cadmium telluride) modules are cheaper and suitable for large installations. CIGS (copper indium gallium selenide) modules offer flexibility and adaptability. A-Si (amorphous silicon) modules are lightweight and useful in specific applications. Other emerging technologies continue to expand market options.</p></p>
