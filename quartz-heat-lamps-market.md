<p><p>The Quartz Heat Lamps market is categorized into three types based on their wavelength: short wave, medium wave, and long wave. Short wave lamps emit high-intensity infrared radiation that penetrates deeply, ideal for therapeutic and industrial applications. Medium wave lamps provide a balanced heat output, suitable for both therapeutic and drying processes. Long wave lamps, with lower intensity, produce gentle heat and are primarily used for comfort heating and in residential settings. Each type serves specific needs in various industries.</p></p>
