<p><p>Real-time Location Systems (RTLS) are technologies used to track and manage assets or individuals in real time. Market types include:</p><p>- **RFID**: Utilizes radio waves to identify and track tags attached to objects.</p><p>- **Wi-Fi**: Leverages existing wireless networks for location tracking through signal triangulation.</p><p>- **Ultrasound**: Uses high-frequency sound waves for precise location information in environments like hospitals.</p><p>- **Infrared**: Employs light signals for short-range tracking, ideal for indoor applications.</p><p>- **Zigbee**: A low-power wireless standard for sensor networks, suitable for large-scale deployments.</p></p>
