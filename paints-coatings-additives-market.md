<p><p>The paints and coatings additives market consists of various types that enhance performance and stability. Biocides protect against microbial growth, ensuring product longevity. Dispersants and wetting agents improve pigment distribution and surface interaction for better application. Stabilizers maintain the consistency and prevent separation of components. Defoamers eliminate unwanted air bubbles during production, while dearaters remove dissolved gases that can affect the quality. Together, these additives optimize paint and coating formulations, ensuring durability and effectiveness.</p></p>
