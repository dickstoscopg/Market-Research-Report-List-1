<p><ul><li>Through Hole</li><li>SMD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The common mode noise filter market is segmented into Through Hole and Surface Mount Device (SMD) types. Through Hole filters are characterized by their pins that pass through the circuit board, offering robust mechanical stability and higher power handling capabilities. In contrast, SMD filters are designed to be mounted directly onto the surface of a PCB, allowing for compact designs and higher assembly efficiency. Both types address electromagnetic interference, crucial for enhancing signal integrity in electronic applications.</p></p>
