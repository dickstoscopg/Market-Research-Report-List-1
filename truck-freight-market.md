<p><p>In the truck freight market, there are several key types: </p><p>1. **Less Than Truckload (LTL)** involves multiple shippers sharing cargo space in one truck, catering to smaller shipments. </p><p>2. **Partial Truckload** is for shipments that are larger than LTL but do not fill an entire truck, optimizing costs for shippers. </p><p>3. **Full Truckload (FTL)** is when a single shipment occupies the entire truck, offering direct transportation with reduced handling. </p><p>4. **Others** may encompass specialized or expedited services, addressing unique freight needs.</p></p>
