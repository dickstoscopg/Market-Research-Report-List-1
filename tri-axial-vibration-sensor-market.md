<p><p>The tri-axial vibration sensor market comprises primarily accelerometers and velocity sensors. Accelerometers measure vibration by detecting changes in acceleration across three axes, making them suitable for dynamic applications. They are widely used in structural health monitoring and machinery diagnostics. Conversely, velocity sensors gauge the speed of vibrating objects, providing critical insights into machinery performance and condition. Both sensor types are essential for predictive maintenance, enabling industries to enhance operational efficiency and reduce downtime through early fault detection.</p></p>
