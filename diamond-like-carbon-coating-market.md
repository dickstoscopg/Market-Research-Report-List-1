<p><p>The Diamond-Like Carbon (DLC) coating market is primarily divided into two types: Hydrogen-Free DLC and Hydrogenated DLC. Hydrogen-Free DLC is characterized by its high hardness, low friction, and excellent wear resistance, making it ideal for applications requiring durability, such as cutting tools and automotive components. In contrast, Hydrogenated DLC contains hydrogen, which improves its flexibility and adhesion to substrates, making it suitable for applications in electronics and medical devices. Both types enhance surface properties, increasing product lifespans and performance.</p></p>
