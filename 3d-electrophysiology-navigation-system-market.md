<p><strong>The 3D Electrophysiology Navigation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-Contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Electrophysiology Navigation System market is divided into two primary types: contact and non-contact. Contact type systems utilize catheters that physically touch the cardiac tissue, providing precise measurements and localized mapping of electrical signals. Conversely, non-contact type systems employ advanced imaging techniques to gather data from the heart without direct contact, allowing for broader mapping of electrical activity. Both types play crucial roles in diagnosing and treating arrhythmias, enhancing the accuracy and efficacy of cardiac interventions.</p></p>
