<p><p>Phase Shift Masks (PSM) are advanced photomasks utilized in semiconductor lithography to enhance the resolution and feature definition on silicon wafers. They operate by introducing a phase difference in the light waves passing through different regions of the mask, improving the contrast of the projected image on the wafer. <p><strong>The Phase Shift Masks PSM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Halftone Mask (Attenuated PSM)</li><li>Levenson Mask (Alternating PSM)</li><li>RimPSM</li><li>Sub ResolutionPSM</li><li>Self-alignmentPSM</li><li>Chrome-lessPSM /All -transparent PSM</li><li>Complex PSM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Phase Shift Masks (PSM) market comprises various types that enhance photolithography in semiconductor manufacturing. Halftone masks, or attenuated PSMs, use partially transparent regions to improve resolution. Levenson masks employ alternating phase shifts for finer detail. RimPSM focuses on edge definition, while Sub-Resolution PSM utilizes features smaller than the wavelength of light to refine image quality. Self-alignment PSM aids in registering layers. Chrome-less PSM or all-transparent PSMs eliminate chrome, allowing for improved transmission, whereas Complex PSM integrates multiple phase shifts for advanced applications.</p></p>
