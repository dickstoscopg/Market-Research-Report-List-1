<p><p>The Aircraft De-icing Vehicles market is primarily categorized into two types: sprayers and spreaders. Sprayers utilize high-pressure systems to evenly distribute de-icing fluids over aircraft surfaces, ensuring effective ice and snow removal. They provide targeted application for critical areas like wings and tail. Spreader vehicles, on the other hand, deploy a wider distribution of de-icing agents, covering larger areas such as runways and taxiways. Both types are essential for maintaining aircraft safety and operational efficiency in winter weather conditions.</p></p>
