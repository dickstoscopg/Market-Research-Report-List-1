<p><p>Home exchange services facilitate travel by allowing participants to swap homes for vacation stays. Non-simultaneous exchanges occur when members stay in the other’s home at different times, offering flexibility. Hospitality exchange involves staying in someone’s home without a direct swap, often with additional amenities or guidance.
