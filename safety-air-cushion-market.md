<p><p>The Safety Air Cushion market consists of two primary types: Air Jet Inflation Type and Fan Inflation Type. Air Jet Inflation Type utilizes high-velocity jets of air to rapidly inflate cushions, providing immediate protection during impacts. In contrast, Fan Inflation Type employs mechanical fans to fill cushions gradually, offering sustained air pressure and cushioning over time. Both types serve to enhance safety in various applications, such as automotive, packaging, and recreational gear, by effectively absorbing and dissipating energy during collisions or falls.</p></p>
